Best Ramen Noodles

Ramen has taken the western world by storm, but what is this dish and what has led to it being so popular all across the world?

The dish of ramen is heavily regionalised, with different variations served in different areas of Japan, although all variations are based on the same broth and noodle concept.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cubed cooked chicken
  • â…› teaspoon dried minced onion, or to taste
  • 4 pinches Creole seasoning
  • 1 dash hot sauce
  • 1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles with seasoning packet
  • 1 slice pepper Jack cheese

Directions

  1. Pour water into a medium saucepan. Add chicken, onion, Creole seasoning, and hot sauce. Bring mixture to a rolling boil. Add noodles and reduce heat to medium; cook for 3 minutes.
  2. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the included noodle seasoning packet and pepper Jack cheese. Let cool slightly before serving.

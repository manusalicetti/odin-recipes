Chunky Italian Spaghetti Sauce

Zesty traditional spaghetti sauce

One of the most important dishes in Italy are the famous spaghetti. We'll combine this ancestral dish with some vanguard flavors to accomplish a perfect sauce for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Combine diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, garlic powder, sugar, parsley, salt, oregano, basil, and pepper in a saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Lower heat to medium-low, cover saucepan, and simmer until flavors blend, about 30 minutes.
